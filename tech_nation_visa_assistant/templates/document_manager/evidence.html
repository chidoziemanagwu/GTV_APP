Evidence Documents

You need to provide evidence documents to support your application. The requirements are:

  • Meet 1 mandatory criterion and 2 optional criteria
  • Provide at least 2 unique documents for each criterion
  • Maximum 10 documents in total
  • Each document limited to 3 A4 pages
  • All evidence must be from the last 5 years

Criteria Categories

Mandatory Criterion

Exceptional Talent

Recognition as a leading talent in the digital technology sector in the last 5 years.

Required Evidence:
  • Employment contracts showing senior technical/leadership roles with significant impact metrics
  • Press coverage of your contributions to major digital products/services
  • GitHub profile showing significant code contributions to major projects
  • Conference presentations/keynotes at major tech events (100+ attendees)

Exceptional Promise

Recognition as having the potential to be a leading talent in the digital technology sector.

Required Evidence:
  • Documentation of technical project leadership roles (even if not senior)
  • Evidence of rapid career progression in tech roles
  • Recognition through awards or competitions
  • Contributions to innovative projects with measurable impact

Optional Criteria (Select 2)

1. Innovation Track Record

Proven track record of innovation as a founder/senior executive or innovative employee

Example Documents:
  • Audited company accounts showing revenue growth and market traction
  • Detailed metrics of product success (user growth, revenue, market share)
  • Granted patents with verifiable ID on Google Patents
  • Technical documentation showing innovative product architecture

2. Recognition Beyond Occupation

Recognition for work outside your immediate occupation that contributes to the advancement of the sector

Example Documents:
  • Open source contributions with metrics (stars, commits, pull requests)
  • Evidence of structured mentoring program participation (12+ months)
  • Main stage speaker at major tech conferences (with attendance proof)
  • Published thought leadership articles in major tech publications

3. Significant Contributions

Technical, commercial or entrepreneurial contributions as a founder, senior executive or employee

Technical Evidence:
  • Core product architecture documentation with impact metrics
  • GitHub contributions showing significant technical impact
Business Evidence:
  • Growth metrics (revenue, users, market share)
  • Investment documents showing raised capital (£25m+)

4. Academic Contributions

Exceptional ability demonstrated through academic research contributions

Example Documents:
  • Published papers in peer-reviewed journals (excluding MSc/undergrad)
  • Evidence of competitive peer-reviewed research grants
  • Merit-based awards from recognized institutions
  • Research supervisor endorsement letters with expert validation
